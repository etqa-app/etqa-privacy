Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Etqa ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Etqa mobile application ("the App").

By using Etqa, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve our services:

Account Information

  • Email address (used for account creation and sign-in)
  • Password (securely encrypted, never stored in plain text)

Usage Data

  • Products scanned (barcode numbers and associated product data)
  • Scan history and favorited products
  • Ingredient search queries

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • General crash and performance logs
What we do NOT collect: We do not collect your location, contacts, photos (other than camera use for barcode scanning), financial information, or any health or biometric data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account management — To create and maintain your account, authenticate your identity, and enable sign-in
  • Core functionality — To scan barcodes, retrieve product ingredients, classify ingredients, and display results
  • Scan history & favorites — To save your scanned products and favorites so you can access them later
  • App improvement — To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve the App's features and performance

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard encryption and security measures. We implement the following protections:

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Firebase Authentication handles all sign-in processes securely
  • Firestore security rules restrict access to ensure users can only view their own data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers — We use Google Firebase for authentication, data storage, and analytics. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy
  • Legal requirements — If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Safety — To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or the public

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Cloud Firestore database, and Analytics (Privacy Policy)

    • Camera Use

    Etqa uses your device's camera solely for scanning product barcodes. Images captured by the camera are processed locally on your device to extract the barcode number. No photos or images are stored, uploaded, or shared. Camera access can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

    Data Retention

    We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your scan history and favorites at any time within the App. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data, please contact us at the email address below, and we will process your request within 30 days.

    Children's Privacy

    Etqa is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

    • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
    • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
    • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data and account
    • Portability — Request a copy of your data in a portable format
    • Objection — Object to certain processing of your data

    To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.
